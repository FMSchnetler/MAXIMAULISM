---
title: "A Forest Birch"
weight: 10
---

{{< poem >}}
On the way to his wooden home,
From his church of wood,
Where he stared at the wooden cross;
Sculpted and embossed.
All chopped, chipped and carved;
We thy children are.
A Russian forest full of trees,
We thy children, just as these.
{{< /poem >}}
