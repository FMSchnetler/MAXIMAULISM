---
title: "Machinations"
weight: 20
---

{{< poem >}}
Grinding gears turn,
The machinations of a man.
Revolutions per minute,
How many devils churned?
How many saints be quickened,
within the walls of my Jericho?
How many sinners overthrown,
Just for a saint to see exile?
My heart is like a battleground,
With ideologues and martyrs,
Where no one ever reigns for long,
But God heals such  disasters
A final turn, a final change,
Something strange and new indeed.
For death is overthrown for all,
And yes, even for me.
{{< /poem >}}
